This is the Front Nest of the Goat

Oct 19

This rain has made the originally hot weather in New York much more refreshing. Today is the weekend. I like to stay at home in rainy days and look out the window at the rain. In the evening, I went out to look for friends. There was already a breath of autumn in the wind, and the leaves on the roadside had fallen a lot, and the colors were particularly beautiful. I decided to go to Central Park one day to see the leaves
